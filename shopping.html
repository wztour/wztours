Shopping Activities

Tappoo Shopping Mall

Explore Fiji’s premium shopping experience with authentic Fiji-made fashion and island wear. Discover vibrant Bula shirts, stylish T-shirts, elegant dresses, traditional sulu and sulu jaba, resort wear, and unique island-inspired clothing perfect for gifts or your Fiji holiday wardrobe

Nadi and Sigatoka Handicraft Market

Discover the beauty of Fiji through authentic handmade crafts and locally made souvenirs. Browse beautifully crafted wood carvings, woven baskets, pottery, jewelry, tapa cloth, shell decorations, coconut crafts, and traditional Fijian artwork created by skilled local artisans.
