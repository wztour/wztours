About Us

What we do Our mission Our goal

At WZ Tours, we provide unforgettable Fiji tour experiences that showcase the beauty, culture, and adventure of Fiji. From scenic highland tours and cultural village visits to waterfalls, mud pools, and city tours, we create memorable journeys for travelers seeking authentic Fijian experiences. Our professional team is dedicated to delivering safe, comfortable, and personalized tours that allow visitors to explore the best places and attractions across Fiji.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional and authentic Fiji tour experiences that connect travelers with the natural beauty, culture, and warm hospitality of Fiji. We are committed to providing reliable, safe, and customer-focused services while creating unforgettable memories for every guest who travels with us.

Our goal is to become one of Fiji’s leading tour providers by offering high-quality tour services, promoting Fiji’s unique destinations and culture, and ensuring customer satisfaction at every step of the journey. We aim to continuously improve our services, support local communities, and provide travelers with meaningful and enjoyable experiences throughout Fiji.

FAQS

Frequently Ask Question

What tours does WZ Tours offer?

WZ Tours offers a variety of Fiji tours including Nadi Half Day Tours (popular), Mud Pool & Hot Spring Tours, Coral Coast Tours, Suva City Tours, Waterfall Tours, Nausori Highlands Tours, and Abaca Hiking & Waterfall Tours. Their tours combine adventure, culture, sightseeing, and relaxation experiences across Fiji.

Does WZ provide hotel pickup and drop-off?

Yes, WZ Tours provides hotel pickup and drop-off services for most tours. Additional transfer charges may apply for pickups outside the Nadi area depending on the distance.

Are entrance fees included in the tour price?

Yes, all standard entrance fees are included as part of the tour package. Additional costs will only apply for optional services or personal expenses such as massages, meals, shopping, drinks, or extra activities not listed in the tour itinerary.

Can cruise ship passengers join your tours?

Yes, WZ Tours offers shore excursion tours for cruise ship passengers with pickup and drop-off arranged according to cruise schedules.

What can I do in a half-day tour in Fiji?

Our half-day tour package is our most popular option, offering a perfect introduction to Fiji’s highlights. It includes visits to popular attractions such as natural mud pools and hot springs, cultural sites, lush tropical gardens, and vibrant local markets. This tour is designed to give you a quick yet rich and memorable experience of Fiji’s beauty, culture, and local lifestyle, all in just a few hours.

Are the tours suitable for children and families?

Yes, many WZ Tours packages are family-friendly and offer discounted rates for children. Some adventure tours may have minimum age recommendations, but most tours are designed for travelers of all ages.
