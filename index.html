Featured Destination

Sri Siva Subramaniya Swami Temple

Sri Siva Subramaniya Swami Temple is the largest Hindu temple in the Southern Hemisphere, known for its colorful South Indian architecture and spiritual importance. Located in Nadi, Fiji, it is a major religious and cultural attraction visited by both worshippers and tourists.



Hot Spring and Mud Pool


Sabeto Hot Springs and Mud Pool is a popular natural attraction in Fiji where visitors can enjoy relaxing geothermal hot springs and therapeutic mud baths. Located near Nadi, it is well known for its healing properties, peaceful surroundings, and unique spa-like experience enjoyed by both locals and tourists.


Nadi Market


Nadi Market is a vibrant local marketplace in the heart of Nadi, Fiji, offering fresh fruits, vegetables, seafood, spices, and traditional Fijian products. It is a popular spot for both locals and tourists to experience Fiji’s culture, friendly atmosphere, and local cuisine.




Nadi Handicraft Market


Nadi Handicraft Market is a popular shopping destination in Nadi, Fiji, where visitors can find traditional Fijian handicrafts, souvenirs, jewelry, wood carvings, clothing, and handmade local products. It offers a great cultural shopping experience for tourists looking to explore Fiji’s art and craftsmanship.


Nadi Town


Nadi is a major town on Fiji’s western side, known as a key tourism hub because it is home to the country’s main international airport. It serves as a gateway to Fiji’s resorts, beaches, and outer islands, and offers a mix of shops, markets, restaurants, and cultural attractions.



Garden of the Sleeping Giants

Garden of the Sleeping Giant is a beautiful botanical garden near Nadi, famous for its vibrant orchids, tropical plants, peaceful walking trails, and stunning mountain scenery. Visitors can relax among lush rainforest paths, lily ponds, and one of Fiji’s largest orchid collections, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and photographers.

First Landing Village


First Landing Beach Resort & Villas is a beautiful beachfront resort located at historic Vuda Point, offering stunning ocean views, traditional Fijian-style accommodation, tropical gardens, and relaxing island hospitality. It is an ideal destination for romantic escapes, family vacations, and enjoying Fiji’s famous sunsets.


Kula Wild Adventure Park

Kula Wild Adventure Park is Fiji’s leading eco-adventure park, offering a mix of wildlife encounters, tropical forest walks, and fun outdoor activities like ziplining and water slides. It is a family-friendly destination focused on conservation and showcasing Fiji’s native plants and animals.



Lawai Pottery Village


Lawai Pottery Village is a traditional Fijian cultural village known for its handmade pottery, where visitors can watch artisans create clay products using age-old techniques. It offers a cultural experience showcasing local craftsmanship, traditions, and the history of pottery-making in Fiji.


Sigatoka Town and Market

Sigatoka is a lively riverside town on Fiji’s Coral Coast, best known for its vibrant local market where visitors can find fresh tropical fruits, vegetables, spices, handicrafts, and traditional Fijian goods. The town offers a genuine glimpse into everyday Fijian life and is a popular stop for travelers exploring the Coral Coast.


Maui Bay


Maui Bay is a peaceful coastal area on Fiji’s Coral Coast, known for its clear turquoise waters, calm lagoon, and beautiful coral reefs. It is a relaxing stop for swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, and enjoying quiet beachfront scenery away from crowded tourist spots.



Fiji Museum


Fiji Museum is Fiji’s main cultural museum in Suva, featuring historical artefacts, traditional crafts, and exhibits that showcase the country’s heritage, culture, and early history.






Suva Capital City


Suva is the capital city of Fiji, located on the southeast coast of Viti Levu. It is the country’s main administrative, commercial, and cultural center, known for its busy markets, colonial architecture, government buildings, and vibrant urban lifestyle.




Sleeping Giant Waterfall


Sleeping Giant Waterfall is a scenic waterfall located in the Sabeto Valley near Nadi, set within lush rainforest surroundings. It is a popular nature attraction offering a refreshing hike, natural swimming spots, and peaceful tropical scenery ideal for adventure and relaxation.



Nausori Highland


Nausori Highlands is a scenic mountainous area near Nadi, known for its rugged landscapes, panoramic viewpoints, and cultural villages. It offers visitors a chance to experience authentic Fijian highland scenery, local traditions, and breathtaking views over the surrounding valleys and coastline.

Nausori Highland Village


Nausori Highlands Village is a traditional highland village experience in the Nausori Highlands near Nadi, where visitors can learn about local Fijian customs, enjoy cultural performances, and experience daily village life surrounded by scenic mountain landscapes.
