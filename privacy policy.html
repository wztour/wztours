Privacy Policy

  Privacy Policy

  At WZ Tours, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services, including making payments via Credit/Master/Visa/AMEX card using Windcave, our   secure payment gateway provider.

  Information We Collect

  We may collect the following information from users:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, billing address, and pickup/drop-off addresses.
  • Payment Information: Credit/Master/Visa/AMEX details during checkout. (Note: We do not store or access your full card information. Payments are processed securely via Windcave.)

  How We Use Your Information

  Your information is used to:

  • Process bookings and payments securely.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Improve website functionality and services.
  • Send service updates or promotional materials (only with your consent).
  • Detect fraud and comply with legal obligations.

  Payment Processing via Windcave

  We use Windcave, a PCI DSS-compliant and secure third-party payment gateway, to process all Credit/Master/Visa/AMEX transactions. Windcave uses encryption and tokenization to protect your card information. We do not store or have access to your complete payment   details at any point.

  You can learn more about Windcave's privacy practices here: https://www.windcave.com/privacy-policy

  Cookies and Analytics

  We use cookies to personalize content, analyze traffic, and improve your experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect some website features.

  Information Sharing

  We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Trusted service providers such as Windcave and Rezdy.
  • Government or legal authorities where required by law.

  Data Security

  We take strong security measures to protect your data, including SSL encryption, firewalls, and secure server hosting. All payment data is transmitted securely to Windcave and handled in compliance with international security standards.

  Your Rights

  You may:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Ask for corrections or deletions.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.

  Third-Party Links

  Our website may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy policies. Please read their policies before submitting any data.

  Children’s Privacy

  We do not knowingly collect data from children.

  Changes to This Policy

  We may update this policy occasionally. All changes will be posted on this page, and the updated date will reflect the effective change.
